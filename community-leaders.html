The GCC Industry Council is a closed peer forum for senior leaders who have shaped Global Capability Centres in India — not a networking group, not a WhatsApp forward chain. A genuine leadership collective.

Membership criteria
Currently serving or has served as a GCC Site Head, Country Head, or equivalent decision-making role
Minimum 15 years of total professional experience
Meaningful contribution to GCC strategy, culture or institutional building
Commitment to peer-only, confidential exchange — no vendor introductions
