2. Personal data we collect

We collect only the data needed to run an access-controlled, audit-grade research portal. Specifically:

2.1 At sign-up (mandatory)

  • Full name
  • Work email address
  • WhatsApp / mobile number (used for account-recovery and verification only, unless you separately opt in to marketing)
  • LinkedIn profile URL (used to verify your professional identity)
  • Current city
  • Years of experience in Global Capability Centers (GCCs)
  • Domain expertise
  • Names of GCCs you have worked with (free-text)
  • A free-text description of your GCC experience and network
  • Password (stored only as a one-way hash by Firebase Authentication; we never see your password)

2.2 Automatically when you use the Portal

  • A one-time verification code emailed to your work address
  • Sign-in timestamps, IP address, and browser user-agent (security log only)
  • The companies you search for and the company profiles you reveal (consumption log used to compute your credit balance)
  • Aggregate page-view counters used for performance debugging
  • A Cloudflare Turnstile bot-protection token at sign-up and on the Enterprise waitlist form (the token is destroyed after verification; no biometric or behavioural fingerprint is retained)

2.3 We do NOT collect

  • Bank account numbers, PAN, Aadhaar, passport, or other government-issued identifiers
  • Photos, videos, or voice recordings
  • Health, biometric, or genetic data
  • Browsing history outside our Portal
  • Sensitive personal data of any kind beyond what is listed in section 2.1

3. Purpose of processing (Notice under Section 5, DPDP Act)

Your personal data is processed for the following specific, lawful purposes only:

  1. Access control. Verify your identity, approve your application, and grant you a credit-based licence.
  2. Service delivery. Show you the GCC research data you are entitled to under your credit balance; reveal company profiles you choose to spend credits on; log your activity for fair-use enforcement.
  3. Account recovery. Email or WhatsApp account-recovery codes when you request them.
  4. Security and fraud prevention. Detect automated abuse, credential sharing, and bulk scraping using Firebase App Check, Cloudflare Turnstile, and rate limits.
  5. Customer support. Respond to your queries sent to admin@gccpros.com.
  6. Statutory compliance. Maintain records required under Indian law (tax, accounting, DPDP).
  7. Marketing (only if you opt in). Send you GCC insights updates on WhatsApp or email. Opt-in is captured separately and can be withdrawn at any time.

We do not use your data for any other purpose. We do not perform automated profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects on you. We do not engage in advertising.

4. Lawful basis (Consent and Legitimate Use)

We rely on your express consent (Section 6, DPDP Act) for all processing described above, captured at the point of sign-up via a clear consent checkbox.

For administrative and statutory record-keeping (e.g. tax records of a paid transaction) we additionally rely on the "Certain Legitimate Uses" basis under Section 7(b), DPDP Act.

You can withdraw consent at any time using the Manage my data flow inside the Portal (described in section 8). Withdrawal does not affect processing that already occurred on the basis of consent.

6. Cross-border transfer

The DPDP Act permits cross-border transfer of personal data except to countries specifically restricted by the Central Government. As of this policy's effective date, the Government of India has not notified any restricted countries.

Our hosting (Supabase) is in Singapore. Some processors (Firebase, Cloudflare, Google) operate globally. By using the Portal you consent to this transfer.

9. Children

The Portal is intended exclusively for working professionals with at least 2 years of GCC experience. We do not knowingly collect personal data of any individual under the age of 18. If you believe a minor has signed up, please notify admin@gccpros.com and we will delete the account.

10. Security

  • Passwords are hashed by Firebase Authentication; never visible to us.
  • All Portal traffic is TLS-encrypted.
  • Database access uses a service-role key held only inside a server-side Supabase Edge Function.
  • Row-Level Security policies prevent any user from reading another user's records.
  • Admin operations require a separate shared secret and an admin email whitelist.
  • We will notify you and the Data Protection Board "without delay" of any personal-data breach that affects you, in accordance with Section 8(6) of the DPDP Act.
