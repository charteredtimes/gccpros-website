Individual Credits FAQ

No subscription. Pay-as-you-go credits.

What do I get for free when I sign up?

500 credits on approval — enough for 5 full company reveals (100 credits each) or 10 search pages (50 credits each, 5 results per page).

What does a search cost vs a reveal?

Search: 50 credits per page of 5 results. Reveal: 100 credits per company — unlocks the full 12-field profile.

What's the monthly reveal cap?

50 unique companies per calendar month (Asia/Kolkata). Re-viewing an already-revealed company is free. Counter resets the 1st of each month.

Do credits expire?

No. Credits don't expire and roll over indefinitely while your account stays active.

How do I buy more credits?

Click Top up in the top bar. 4 INR packs (₹200 / ₹500 / ₹950 / ₹2,250) and 4 USD packs ($2.50 / $6 / $11.50 / $27) for 200 / 500 / 1,000 / 2,500 credits. Processed by Razorpay. Credits land instantly.

Can I get a refund?

Credits are non-refundable once added. For billing errors or service failures on our side, email admin@gccpros.com.

Do I earn credits for anything?

Yes — accepted corrections earn 20 credits. Submit via the "Suggest a correction" button on any reveal card. Suggesting a new GCC can also earn credits (variable).

Is my activity logged and watermarked?

Yes. Every reveal is watermarked diagonally with your email + timestamp. Every spend is logged in your account transaction history (Statement button in the top bar).

Can my team share one account?

No — Individual access is licensed per named person. For multi-seat access, see the Enterprise waitlist at the top of the page.
