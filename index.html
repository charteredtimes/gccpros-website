Community Charter

A professional institution,
not a commercial platform

"The GCC Industry Council is a peer community of professionals in full-time employment across India's Global Capability Centres. Membership carries no commercial obligation. The Council does not represent any employer and operates as an independent, non-commercial knowledge-sharing and advocacy body."

🤝
Peer-to-peer only
No consulting engagements, no vendor relationships, no commercial introductions through the Council.
🏛️
Employer-neutral
Members participate as individual professionals, not as representatives of their organisations.
🔒
Members-only discretion
Discussions within the community are shared among members in good faith. Members are expected to exercise professional discretion.
