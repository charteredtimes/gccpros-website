📖 How to publish

1
Fill in the form above and click Generate JSON Entry
2
Copy the generated JSON
3
Open blog.json on GitHub → click the pencil ✏️ to edit → paste your entry inside "articles": [ at the top of the list
4
Click Commit changes — your article appears on the insights page within 2 minutes
5
To invite a council member to write: share them this tool, they generate their JSON, send it to you, and you paste it in
